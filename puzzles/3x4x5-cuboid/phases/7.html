3x4x5 Cuboid - Phase 7

1 2 3 4 5 6 7

Step A

Case 1

one or both of front and back middle centers are not correct. Go to Step B.

-

Case 2

Both front and back middle centers are incorrect. Swap centers.

u2 Rw Lw d2 Rw Lw

Step B

Case 1

back down middle center is correct. Go to Step C.

-

Case 2

back down middle center is incorrect. Rotate guide until it is correct.

x2 or y2

Step C

Case 1

front up middle center is correct. Go to Step D.

-

Case 2

front up middle center is incorrect. Swap it for correct center.

F Rw u2 Rw F Rw u2 Rw

Step D

Case 1

front down middle center is correct. Go to Step E.

-

Case 2

front down middle center is incorrect. Swap it for correct center.

Rw u2 Rw F Rw u2 Rw F

Step E

Case 1

front up left center is correct. Go to Step F.

-

Case 2

front up left center is incorrect. Swap it for correct center.

r u2 r u2 (1x or 2x)

Step F

Case 1

front down right center is correct. Go to Step G.

-

Case 2

front down right center is incorrect. Swap it for correct center.

r d2 r d2 (1x or 2x)

Step G

Case 1

one or both of front up right center and front down left center are incorrect. Go to Step H.

-

Case 2

front up right center and front down left centers are both incorrect. Swap them for correct centers.

F r u2 r u2 F r u2 r u2

Step H

Case 1

All centers correct. Puzzle solved.

-

Case 2

back down right correct and front down left incorrect. Swap it for correct center.

Lw d Rw u' Lw u Rw d' r d Rw u' Lw u Rw d' Lw r

Case 3

back down right correct and front up right incorrect. Swap it for correct center.

F Lw d Rw u' Lw u Rw d' r d Rw u' Lw u Rw d' Lw r F
