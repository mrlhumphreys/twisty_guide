Parity issues

Sometimes some odd cases can appear. Edges might need twisted around.

Case 1

Only one pair of edges not oriented correctly.

(r2 B2 U2 l) (U2 r' U2 r) (U2 F2 r F2) (l' B2 r2) Show

Case 2

Two opposite edge pairs not oriented correctly.

Uw2 Lw2 U2 l2 U2 Lw2 Uw2 Show

Case 3

Two adjacent edge pairs not oriented correctly.

L2 D Fw2 Lw2 F2 l2 F2 Lw2 Fw2 D' L2 Show

Case 4

Two adjacent corners not oriented correctly.

Uw2 Lw2 U2 l2 U2 Lw2 Uw2 F' U' F U F R' F2 U F U F' U' F R Show

Case 5

Two opposite corners not oriented correctly.

Uw2 Lw2 U2 l2 U2 Lw2 Uw2 R U' L U2 R' U R L' U' L U2 R' U L' U Show
