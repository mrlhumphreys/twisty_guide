Third Layer Corners

Slot the remaining corners in the third layer, similar to the first layer.

Down edge facing right.

R U R'

Down edge facing front.

F' U' F

Down edge facing up.

R U' R' U2 R U R'

Fourth Layer Edges

Slot the edges in the fourth layer, similar to the second layer.

Case 1

Edge needs to be rotated clockwise around center.

(U R U' R') (U' F' U F)

Case 2

Edge needs to be rotated anti-clockwise around center.

(U' F' U F) (U R U' R')
