Fifth Layer Orient Edges

Match up the edges on the last layer and make a star shape.

Case 1

Two adjacent edges facing out.

F (U R U' R') F'

Case 2

Two opposite edges facing out.

F (R U R' U') F'

Fifth Layer Position Edges

Cycle the edges around until they are in the right place.

Case 1

Cycle three edges clockwise.

(R U R' U) (R U3 R' U)

Case 2

Cycle three edges anti-clockwise.

(U' R U2 R') (U' R U' R')

Fifth Layer Position Corners

Cycle the corners around until they are in the right place.

Case 1

Cycle three corners clockwise.

(L' U2 R U'2) (L U2 R' U'2)

Case 2

Cycle three corners anti-clockwise.

(R U'2 L' U2) (R' U'2 L U2)

Fifth Layer Orient Corners

Run an algorithm to rotate each of the corners until they face the right way.
