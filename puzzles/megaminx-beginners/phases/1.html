Star Shape

Match up the five edges with one center piece to form a star.

Only Case

Move edges to down layer to make a star shape.

-

First Layer

Slot the corners into the first layer.

Case 1

Down edge facing right.

R U R'

Case 2

Down edge facing front.

F' U' F

Case 3

Down edge facing up.

R U' R' U2 R U R'

Second Layer

Slot the edges into the second layer.

Case 1

Edge needs to be rotated clockwise around center.

(U R U' R') (U' F' U F)

Case 2

Edge needs to be rotated anti-clockwise around center.

(U' F' U F) (U R U' R')
