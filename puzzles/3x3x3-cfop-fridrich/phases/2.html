Match the edge with the corner.

Case 1

Edge placed but not oriented correctly.

(R U' R') Dw (R' U2) (R U2) (R' U R)

Case 2

Rotate edge clackwise to match corner.

(U R U' R') Dw' (L' U L)

Rotate edge anti-clackwise to match corner.

Match the corner with the edge.

Corner with bottom facing front.

Corner in correct position with bottom facing front.

Rotate corner and match with edge.

Slot edge in place anti-clockwise and rotate corner.

(R U') (R' U) (R U' R')
