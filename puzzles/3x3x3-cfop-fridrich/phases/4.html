3x3x3 Cfop Fridrich - Phase 4

1 2 3 4

Only corners not oriented.

Rotate corners around.

Case 1

Cycle 3 corners clockwise

x (R' U R') D2 (R U' R') D2 R2

Case 2

Cycle 3 corners anti-clockwise

x R2 D2 (R U R') D2 (R U' R)

Case 3

Cycle 2 pairs of corners parallel

x' (R U') (R' D) (R U R' D') (R U R' D) (R U') (R' D')

Only edges not oriented.

Rotate edges around.

Case 1

Cycle 3 edges anti-clockwise.

(R U' R U) (R U) (R U') (R' U' R2)

Case 2

Cycle 3 edges clockwise.

(R2 U) (R U R' U') (R' U') (R' U R')

Case 3

Swap opposite edges.

(M2 U) (M2 U2) (M2 U) (M2)

Case 4

Swap adjacent edges in parallel.

(M2 U) (M2 U) (M' U2) (M2 U2) (M' U2)
