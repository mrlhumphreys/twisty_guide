Skewb - Layer by Layer Method

Solve First Layer

Pick a face and match corners with it.

Case 1

Rotate last corner clockwise.

L' F' L F

Case 2

Rotate last corner anti-clockwise.

R F R' F'

Solve Remaining Faces

Cycle three faces at a time until faces are positioned right.

Case 1

Rotate three faces (Left, Right & Down) clockwise.

(R' D' R D) (L D L' D')

Case 2

Rotate three faces (Right, Left & Down) anti-clockwise.

(L D L' D') (R' D' R D)

Solve Last Layer Corners

Cycle and rotate corners until fully solved.

Case 1

Two corners are correctly facing down and two corners are facing back. Rotate other corners.

(L' F L F') x 4

Case 2

Two corners are incorrectly facing right. Rotate all corners.

(L' F L F') x 2
